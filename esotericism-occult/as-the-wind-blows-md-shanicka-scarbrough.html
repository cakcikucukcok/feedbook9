In As the Wind Blows, seven women share their journeys of love, lust, infidelity, infertility, premature childbirth, heartache, mental illness, and unhealthy relationships. As each woman shares the intimate details of her life, you will find yourself experiencing the full spectrum of human emotion, becoming engrossed in their trials and finding common ground with your own. While each woman’s story is unique, all display the beautiful quality of resiliency: the innate strength to keep pushing, to keep learning, to keep believing, to keep growing, and most of all, to keep triumphing despite painful circumstances. 

This book was written to help heal broken hearts and to share the good news that you are not alone. At times, it may seem like all is lost and the pressure is just too much to bear, but remember that God is in control—and as women, it is etched in our DNA to endure and overcome.
