Everyone on this planet is a unique individual. There is no other person in the universe like you. Only you and you alone know what is best for you and your highest good. Insights from the Heart offers valuable insights about how using positive affirmations and incorporating the teachings of Louise Hay helped author Tania Thornton to change her life.

Insights from the Heart presents a collection of essays and affirmations written by Thornton to empower us to embrace who we are and to accept all the blessings the universe longs to bestow upon us. In a warm, comforting, and encouraging tone, Thornton addresses many situations we encounter in our daily lives, guiding us through the steps of embracing these sometimes difficult life experiences. She believes that blessings and loving messages lie within you and that you deserve the very best in the world; you are worth it.

A new day is dawning. I open my eyes. I look out the window and see the sun rise. Feeling scared and uncertain that life passes me by, I take a deep breath and let out a sigh.

As the tears wash away all my hurts and my fears And start to heal all my wounds from over the years, I am on a new path now, one I can create. My hopes and my dreams, I hand them to fate...
