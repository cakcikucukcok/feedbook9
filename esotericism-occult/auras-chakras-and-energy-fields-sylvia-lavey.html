"Auras, Chakras, and Energy Fields: What They Are To You and How Your Angels and Guides Work Through Them" is a quick-read.

Like those that are reflected by the sunlight through stained glass windows in a church, synagogue, or temple.

Your aura and chakras are subtle energies that your angels and guides work through to help you and to guide you on many different levels.

Eating foods that are the color of certain chakras will help to strengthen that chakra and your aura.
