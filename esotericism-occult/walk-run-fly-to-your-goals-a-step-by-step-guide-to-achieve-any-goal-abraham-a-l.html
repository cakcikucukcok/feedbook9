The journey towards a goal, particularly the big ones, are multifaceted.

'WALK, RUN, FLY TO YOUR GOALS: A simple step by step guide to achieve any goal' gives you a glimpse and an overview of all the skills and steps that are needed.

The book is not meant to be read in one sitting. Each lesson on a particular chapter should ideally be internalised after reading and reflected upon for at least a day. Some readers may need to take a slightly longer period of time. The various chapters of the book represents different stages of a person's journey towards his or her goals and should be first read in chronological order. At the end of the book, there is a chapter which deals specifically with specific segments of your journey which may need more attention.
