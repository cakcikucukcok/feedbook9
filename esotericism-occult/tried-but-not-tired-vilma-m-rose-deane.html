In this book, I have shared with you real experiences from my life and that of my familys. You will discover how God proved Himself true and faithful to us every step of the way. He took us through fiery trials which included sickness and death, and made us victorious; so that our triumphs out-numbered our trials! I have also shared some points in the introduction that I think we should consider as we go through our trials and tests. These points include:

1. We are living in a world where evil exists.

2. We are targets for the enemy.

3. It is God Who is on trial and not us.

4. God will fight for us.

5. We have a purpose to fulfill.
