As a child we dreamed and lived life in WONDER. Then as we grew up we learned to make a living instead of learning to live. Day in and day out, feeling excited when Friday was finally here and deflated when Monday came. This book inspires readers to live out their dreams. When we love life, we become life and then we are able to share life’s greatness with others!

As you become motivated, inspired, and have belief in yourself you will realize that at your core, you are a miracle! You have greatness in you and you have an amazing purpose for your life. You can achieve and do all that you put your mind to. Nothing can stand in your way and you have all that you need to do what you desire. As a female millennial, I want to share a vision of hope for others that have let stereotypes and other people’s opinions get in their way. When it comes down to it, you can be anything you want to be. You were created to do what makes you happy!

Learn ways to become organized so you don’t feel like you are always getting ready to get ready. You will transform your life in every aspect, including your health, finances, and professionally!

Connect with others in a way that is authentic and intentional. Your life will be ever changed by brightening someone’s day. In a world of overwhelming technology you will become more present with your family and friends making the most of the special moments with them. When we stop to smell the roses and see the wonder in our lives it creates a positive fulfilling life.

What if…just what if…your wildest elephant size dreams could come true! They can and will, learn simple ways to take action so you can start living your dreams each day. Whether you want to be a leader, entrepreneur or change agent you can make a difference in your life and in the world! But, you are saying; how can I make a difference? Take a deep dive to discover all the possibilities!
