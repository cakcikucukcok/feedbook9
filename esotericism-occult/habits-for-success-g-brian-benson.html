One of Brian Benson's specialties is helping people bring balance and centeredness to their lives. He believes that having a balanced life is the first step in creating your dream life and living out your true purpose. The things that keep us in balance? are the things that help us feel alive, help us feel love, help us feel good about ourselves, and excited about. One has to have a solid foundation or anchor to build upon; to listen to your intuition, create, and manifest. Otherwise, your daily life be off balance and you will be inconsistent and scattered. In Habits for Success, Brian offers a meaningful yet light-hearted approach to finding the balance that we all strive to find in our busy lives. He believes that living a balanced and centered lifestyle combined with a loving acceptance and belief in yourself is key to speeding up the process of attaining and accomplishing all of your goals and dreams. Things ome much easier and flow much more readily when we are in balance. Using his own story, the author shares his journey on how finding more balance in his life has lead to unforeseen abundance in the form of creativity, fulfillment and intuitive guidance. Using tools that helped him personally as well as expanding on his original list, Brian has created a blueprint to help readers everywhere lead a happier, consistent and authentic life. Readers will learn: Why it’s important for you to have balance in your life. Why it’s hard to stay in life balance. 35 different fun, informative and life changing ways to establish balance in your life on an ongoing basis. Techniques on how to identify when you are out of balance and how to counter it.? How to start living a life of authenticity and true-fulfillment. How you can strengthen your intuitive skills by implementing more life balance into your life. How establishing balance in your life will increase creativity and productivity. That living a more balanced life will lead to healthier relationships with loved ones and friends. That living a more balanced life will lead to increased personal awareness.
