Past life personalities continue to live...

Memory’s Journey revises our concepts of karma. It shows through past life memories that what we experience as karmic changes over our many lifetimes, and teaches how karma is always the unsettled emotion that stands in the way of our journey toward love. It reveals precisely, through the lens of actual readings and regressions, how past life drama becomes the centerpiece of our current life.  It documents how the confusing emotions of our current life may have come to us over a series of lifetimes, and shows how past life personalities continue to live in surprising ways; how they often go on to evolve and heal beyond the boundaries of our present lifetime.

Janae provides a detailed past life regression script you can record to successfully discover your own past lives, as well as a system of energy healing through the emotional vibrations of color.
