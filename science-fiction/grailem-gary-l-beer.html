Grailem was one of Mankind's last hopes to finally achieve immortality. Created in a test tube he had been bred specifically to be fully integrated into a mechanical body. Forced into a spacecraft he is blasted into space where the spacecraft explodes and he spends an eternity drifting through space. Coming across an ancient alien race, who is more of a threat to Mankind than he ever was, he fights to save humanity from these evil aliens. To live forever has been Man's dream almost since time began; would immortality bring the happiness that we all desire?
