A group of teenagers in an isolated piece of paradise are increasingly disenchanted with the failure of their elders to provide a loving and meaningful life. Throw in an ageing intergalactic rock band who are looking for their next gig a band whose members include a costume-loving hermaphrodite, Bear who can play a mean riff on the steel guitar and morph into a savage Ursine creature when needed, and a blue-skinned Saurian drummer with a mysterious past.
