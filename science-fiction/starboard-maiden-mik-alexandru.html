If you woke up with a mind full of math and physics equations, without remembering who you are or what anything around you was, would you be able to save humanity? This is the question army veteran Jake Rhodes is faced with. He must figure out why he remembers all this and what he can do to set things right. But it's not as easy as solving a puzzle. Jake soon discovers that advanced scientific theories, glimpses of an alternate timeline, and complicated engine schematics are not his only problems. He must outmaneuver covert government agents, fleets of hostile alien vessels, and his own psyche in order to reach full understanding. It is up to Jake, his friends, and a mysterious vanishing girl to make sure humanity endures.
