After their ship crashes, a group of survivors band together as they try to find a way to escape their fatal destination, but things are not what they seem; perhaps there was more to the crash than they thought. Things might have been straightforward had they not encountered the threat of the Terrors, the vile beasts that inhabit the planet. Are the Terrors as indestructible as they seem at first? Or can the group find a way to overcome them? Stryker: Fatal Destination is the first in a three-part series; keep an eye out for the second instalment, Stryker: Half Steel Alpha.
