Raikon is a mercenary fighting to build a name for himself in the Chaos Armada military and is thrown into a situation that involves the fate of his planet Cressen. While originally leaving his home behind so he could become a soldier he quickly finds himself in battles that threatens everyone across his planet. The military he serves under specializes in dealing with the supernatural ranging from demon legions to monsters and entities.

Once he finds out more about his family’s blood lineage and the secret powers they hold things turn dangerous quickly. He soon discovers his family’s powers but not before his own friends hunt him down for a crime he didn’t commit.

As he ventures into the world of Cressen he finds out about an ancient evil known as Skurge that has the power to destroy the world with ease. As he tries to find out more about this power he falls into a deep darkness that threatens to take control of his soul and destroy everything he loves and cares about.

After finding out who betrayed him he sets his sights on taking down the tyrant who seeks to control the evil godlike power and govern the world as his own. Raikon is forced to make drastic decisions on his journey to uncover the only power that has a chance of saving his planet.

Raikon’s adventure takes him across the massive world of Cressen that is ruled by its three moons. These celestial bodies contain more evil that Skurge will release upon the world. His finest hour will have him facing down the evil that wants to devour Cressen in a battle of epic proportions.
