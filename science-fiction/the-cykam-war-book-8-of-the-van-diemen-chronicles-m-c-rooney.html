In Tasmania Jack Dayton, after his life altering experience with an entity from the planet Mars, has passed on his uncontrolled mind reading abilities to his daughter and grandson. But this is not an ability welcomed by the everyday public of Hobart as they believe privacy is a basic human right and the family is quickly ostracised.
