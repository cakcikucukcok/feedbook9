Doctor Laura Hood has barely managed to recover from her adventurous romp with her patient Lamar in the morning.  As she soaks in a warm bath, she can't help but want to feel his manhood again.  Her body grows hot and bothered at the thought and she steps out of the bath to pick up Lamar's business card.

She musters up the courage to call him and invites him over.  Both Lamar and Laura know exactly why she's calling him to come over.  While she nervously waits, she tries to loosen herself up with her fingers.

Unfortunately, her neighbor interrupts her session to see how she's doing.  Laura tries to kindly get her to go away and as she succeeds a car pulls up in front of her house.  She trembles as she watches Lamar step out of the car, his big muscular frame exciting her, even from a distance.  Surprisingly, another large black man steps out from the passenger seat.

Lamar introduces the other black man as his friend, Devon, who's just arrived back home after finished up a tour with the army.  She's amazed by how large Devon is, somehow more muscular and taller than Lamar.  Once the two step in, her towel comes off and the excitement begins...
