Alexandria, an adventurous, self-sufficient, self-assured young woman, is stranded in France after WWII breaks out in Europe. She has acquired a number of foundlings that she has to get to safety when she discovers that the world as she knows it is just an illusion and that there are other species living among usunbeknown to all but a very few humansas her journey continues and she becomes more aware of the reality, of this new world she had no idea existed, falls in love, and narrowly escapes from situations she merely stumbles into due to her ignorance, gets accepted into a family of another species as part of the family, as one of them. Life as she knew it was to be no more. With what she knew now, she could no longer be a part of the world she knew. But what she finds . . . will it be the undoing of her, or will she triumph?
