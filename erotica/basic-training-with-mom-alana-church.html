“Hello? Can I help you?”

“His mother? I like it. So what’s the roleplay? Do you find us screwing, then get jealous?”

She dug her hands into her scalp, tempted to scream. “Listen,” she said. “Can we get in my car and talk?”

A wicked smile drifted over the Latina woman’s lips. “Sure, honey.” She set her hand on her chest, just above her breasts. “Whatever you want.”
