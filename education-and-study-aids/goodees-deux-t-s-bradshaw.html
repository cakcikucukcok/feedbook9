About the Book Little listeners, active and visual learners get ready for more jumping, running, rolling, and learning with The Goodees. Wollie the English speaking frog, Alex the Spanish speaking dog, PePe the French speaking pig, and Stuie the Chinese speaking bear are back to share what they know and love to do with all of you. Goodees Deux is a fun, fantastic follow-up to The Goodees book. Wollie the Goodee Frog teaches young learners beginning English words. Alex the Goodee Dog exposes young learners to beginning Spanish words. PePe the Goodee Pig helps young learners with beginning French words. Stuie the Goodee Bear teaches young learners beginning Mandarin words. Goodees Deux also teach young learners beginning geometry, shape recognition, size difference, animal print recognition, identifying foods and much more. Young learners also practice handwriting skills and how to say "please" and "thank you" in four different languages. Children, parents, and educators will have lots of fun with this delightful book that offers something for the young visual, auditory, and kinesthetic learner.
