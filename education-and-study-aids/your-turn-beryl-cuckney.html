keeping legal, healthy and safe

problem solving

using your manager

using technology

getting people to listen

money and fraud.

Your Turngives you half an hour a week with your manager, finding out answers to questions buzzing around in your head. You will be amazed how quickly 26 weeks pass, and at the end you work out your management competence.

But Your Turn is not only about surviving 26 weeks. Each week you FROG' take charge of the subject and do something to make your management grip secure and firm. You start to become someone that others will take notice of; you start to become influential.
