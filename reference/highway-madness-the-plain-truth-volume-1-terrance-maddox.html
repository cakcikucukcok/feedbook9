Accidents are caused, they dont just happen. Road hazards, obstacles, or someone simply not paying attention is a threat to humanity. Reading and learning the contents of this book could save the lives of you and others by knowing these hazards and how to protect yourself from them.

Be sure to read Terrance T. Maddoxs other books in this unique, and often delightfully humorous, series he calls, Highway Madness, The Plain Truth.
