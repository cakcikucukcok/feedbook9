The Art of Speaking and Storytelling

Do you have something to say but you are not sure how to say it?

Are you looking for a way to create a memorable experience for your audience?

Do you need the secret to creating and sharing stories that move people?

Then you need Speaking Savvy -  The Art of Speaking and Storytelling.

This book is full of tools and tricks on how you can be a far more effective storyteller and speaker.

If you are a little shy or don’t know where to start - have no fear because that is where I was!

I used to be very shy and afraid of public speaking. It took a life-changing illness to realise that it doesn't help anyone for me to remain scared to speak out when I have a message worth sharing.

So now I am driven to help others to use their voice so they can speak up and share their stories and messages in a way that is powerful and compelling. 

The first step is to learn some of the tips, tools, and tricks so that you can confidently speak in front of an audience any size. 

The next step is to put those tools into action, and I have some extra resources including videos and templates to help you do that 

Speaking Savvy - The Art of Speaking and Storytelling will help you share your message. 

To make sure you get a real kick start down the path, I have created an initial launch package that includes some extra resources for you.

Some of the speaking elements it covers include:

How to turn 'fear' into 'fierce.'

How to create a speech using a simple ABC framework

How to plan a successful presentation

How to use your voice as a powerful tool

How to enhance your communication with non-verbal cues

Understand how to use storytelling a compelling medium,

and much more
