An immediately usable guide to the many ways Music Therapy can be used on its own and in combination with other disciplines. Based on the author's 24 years of experience, this book gives many concrete examples of innovative programs and their results. They involve Music Therapy on its own, and also combined with Speech Therapy, drama, costume, visual aids, recording, instrument making, and song writing. These proven blends are applicable to adults with a variety of conditions including Multiple Sclerosis, stroke, Parkinsons, Lou Gehrig's, dementia, Cerebral Palsy, Huntington's and brain injury in adults age 25 to 100 years plus. Details of a specially designed software application used as an aid in organizing repertoire are included.
