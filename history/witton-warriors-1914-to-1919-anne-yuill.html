WITTON WARRIORS IS THE STORY OF WITTON LE WEAR, A SMALL RURAL VILLAGE IN COUNTY DURHAM, ENGLAND, DURING THE GREAT WAR. IT GIVES INSIGHT INTO THE MEN WHO LEFT THE VILLAGE RAILWAY STATION IN HIGH SPIRITS, TO FIGHT FOR THEIR KING, COUNTRY AND FREEDOM.
