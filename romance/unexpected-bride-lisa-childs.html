Always a bridesmaid? 

Only her best friend's wedding could bring Abby Hamilton back to her Michigan hometown. But when the bride runs away, the reception turns into an unexpected homecoming party for Cloverville's prodigal daughter. Everyone's happy to see Abbyexcept Clayton McClintock, the bride's straight-arrow older brother and Abby's unrequited crush. 

Where Abby goes, trouble follows. But Clayton has to admit the adult Abby is very different from the rebellious eighteen-year-old who left without a backward glance. She runs a successful business and is a single mother to an adorable four-year-old, who is quickly stealing Clayton's heart. Suddenly a man with no intentions of settling down is thinking about making a home and a family
