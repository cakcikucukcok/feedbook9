Pam told her story and because she made it to the other side of grief upon grief, I knew that I could too. Having the privilege of then getting to know her personally, I can say that her writing is genuine and authentic to who she is and that she is a living testimonynot just a written one.

I am beyond excited to continue reading with the release of this new book. I am a life that was changed. ~Leigh~
