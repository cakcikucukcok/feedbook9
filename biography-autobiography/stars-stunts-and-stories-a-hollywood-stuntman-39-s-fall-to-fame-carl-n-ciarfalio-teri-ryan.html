Veteran Hollywood stuntman Carl Ciarfalio takes readers inside the celebrity-filled world of blockbuster entertainment with this easy-to-read collection of short stories. Stars, Stunts and Stories: One Man's Fall to Fame is the memoir of veteran Hollywood stuntman Carl Ciarfalio. Over the last 40 years Carl has worked with major celebrities including Tom Cruise, Brad Pitt, Woody Harrelson, Mila Kunis, Clint Eastwood, Keifer Sutherland, Jennifer Garner, Carol O'Connor, Lou Ferrigno and Tom Selleck.?

As Carl puts it, "Stuntmen and stuntwomen have the coolest jobs in the industry. We get to fight, fall, burn up, get blown up, kill and get killed. We crash cars, boats, motorcycles and planes, work in tandem with your favorite stars, and take orders from your favorite directors."  

Readers will travel behind the scenes, hearing insider anecdotes about mega-stars, gnarly stunts and will receive useful insights and advice about the unique and exciting world of stunts.

Carl’s life off the set has been just as interesting. He opens up about a teen pregnancy, single fatherhood, surviving a nasty Hollywood divorce and finally finding true love—plus the time he got run over for real by a criminal driving a half-ton truck!

As compelling as your favorite action movie, "Stars, Stunts, and Stories" will have you turning the pages...and glued to your seat!
