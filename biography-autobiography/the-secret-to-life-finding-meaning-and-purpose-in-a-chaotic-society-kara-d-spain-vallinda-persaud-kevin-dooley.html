Part Memoir, part guide to life, and how to find meaning and purpose in an often chaotic society. The author touches on small town, southern life, and how that influenced her life and view of the world. This book takes a hard look at some of the falsehoods that have permeated our society, and how to find meaning and purpose regardless. It also attempts to dissect the taboo subjects of life, such as racism, death, religion, relationships, and education. It is a book that takes a poignant look at life, society, and the bumps and bobbles we all experience along the way.
