Learn the Inspirational Story Behind One of Football's Greatest Cornerbacks! This short & unauthorized biography of Richard Sherman highlights his accomplished career. Ever since he entered the league with a chip on his shoulder, Richard Sherman has cemented himself as one of the greatest cornerbacks to ever play in the NFL. In this book, we'll explore Richard's journey to the national football league along with the impact that he has had on the game. Whether you are a fan of the Seahawks, or simply a fan of the game of football, Richard's story is an inspiring one of how one cornerback beat all the odds to ultimately became one of the most prized players of his class. Here is a preview of what you'll find in this book: Childhood and Early Life High School Years Quick Facts About Him College Years Richard's NFL Career Richard's Legacy And Much More.... Get the ultimate story behind the journey of how Richard Sherman became one of the most dominant figures in the history of football!
