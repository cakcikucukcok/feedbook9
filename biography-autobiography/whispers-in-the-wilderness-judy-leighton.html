After suffering terrifying flashbacks of fear and pain for decades, Judy Leighton, pain and stress consultant, was facing death for six months with an energy-sapping illness with no known cure. One night she prayed and received an answer that helped her develop a healing method. Using this simple-to-use method, her flashbacks vanished after ten daysthe fear and pain from her memories were gone. After two more weeks, her illness also disappeared.

Judys mission is to share her healing method, and she has authored three books. In this book she tells her surprising healing journey and relates numerous spiritual adventures, showing how ordinary days unfold into the extraordinary.

Her method answers why so many people have made unhealthy or unwise choices, even when they knew better. This book presents a method for people to recognize and release themselves from barriers to making wise and healthy choices.
