Defiant, self-centered, confident and incredibly young. Could these attributes describe a hero saving the universe?

In this tale, our heroine’s struggle for survival begins after an outlandish accident at the Grand Canyon. Days filled with explorations, warring factions and pursuit of an unattainable relationship keep our teenager plenty busy. What she lacks in experience and skills she compensates for with luck and a vivid imagination. While fighting responsibility with ingenuity and unshakable bravery, our teenager embraces her new reality and becomes fully immersed in an alien society. This story covers the emotional roller coaster that defines many teenagers from the singular view of the main character, who has mastered and armed herself with her favorite weapon at her disposal--sarcasm.
