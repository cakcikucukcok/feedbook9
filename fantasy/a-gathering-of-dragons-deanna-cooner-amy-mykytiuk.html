Lured by the need for control over her own life and a craving for respect as a woman, Barbara Troye leaves her family principles behind when she enters college. When she discovers the dragon has followed her with evil intent her anger grows as well as her fear. She no longer believes in a personal God but only in her own abilities. When her own devices fail, she feels only confusion, even her biblical knowledge fails her.  Can she be rescued before she falls for the lies of a demon set upon her destruction?
