"Seek the hamalakh, the sorcerer, the mastak; seek Tamara, Hibu, Karejakal. Three to lead, three to save, against the darkness..."

The dying words of a renown Oracle set four people on a quest to discover the secret to saving the world.

Allaha - A monastic knight of the Order of Aisha, Fallen of the Mountain. Stoic and resolute, she is the guardian of the others.

Tamara - A Menori hamalakh, she can sense the emotions of those around her. A free spirited girl that is sometimes wise beyond her years.

Hibu - Journeyman Sorcerer of the Zho, a royal line in his homeland. An ever curious young man with powerful magic.

Karejakal - A Tibu mastak orphaned by the death of his clan, able to communicate with the spirits of the dead.

Chosen by fate, four strangers struggle to find answers and discover how they are meant to save their world.
