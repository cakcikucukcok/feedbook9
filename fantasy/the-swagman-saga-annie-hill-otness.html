The Australian heritage embraces beginnings

when this vast nation was forged by men and women

in chains in the furnace of brutality and bloodshed.

The Swagman Saga celebrates a uniquely Australian icon,

the myth and legend of the archetypical journeyman

who observes and transforms the landscapes he traverses.

The Swagman journeys endlessly until his track crosses

that of Matilda, the free-born witch, daughter of a

line of Cornish Cunning Women - with a gift and burden

of a magic trunk. Tai Foon, the Golem Chinese Warrior

protects her from the Terrible Shapechanger, Captain Sharman,

who relentless pursues her and aboriginal survivors.

A cast of fantastic characters are tangled in a storyline

of the transcendent battle of good against evil -

and the pursuit of freedom in a turbulent

and remote settlement in an ancient land.
