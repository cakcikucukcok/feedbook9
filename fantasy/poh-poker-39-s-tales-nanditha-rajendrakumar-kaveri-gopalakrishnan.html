Poh and Pokers Tales takes you on a merry-go-round of fun adventures in the animal world. Meet your hostsPoh, the elephant, and Poker, the monkeythe happy-go-lucky duo who are so different and yet the best of friends. Whether it is dancing at a party with fancy flamingos and a fiery-looking lion or whizzing by on rides at carnivals, Poh and Poker have an eventful life packed with fun and adventure. Along the way, they (and the tiny readers) learn to appreciate the value of companionship, kindness, and persistence. With a radiant forest as the backdrop and a starring cast of forest and farm animals, Poh and Pokers Tales is sure to mesmerize its readers.
