WOULD YOU KNOW IF YOU WERE GOING INSANE? This is what Syden is faced with every single day, while she learns how to deal with growing up in a world, it becomes apparent that loved ones don't always have her best interest at heart. The Night Birds is an emotional rollercoaster ride through childhood, adolescence, and the struggles of becoming a woman. Follow the story of Syden's search for truth as the lines between fantasy, imagination, great love, and deep loss, merge with the painful reality of an unexplained illness and heartbreaking dissolution. In a world of temptation and lies, will Syden be able to define the difference between madness and truth..., before it's too late? Some nights are like dreams where everything is happily beyond your control. A good dream is when you don't want it to end, but if it does, and if you're lucky, you get to be the one who walks away.
