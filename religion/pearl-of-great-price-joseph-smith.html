"Pearl of Great Price" is one of the four holy books of the Mormon church in addition to the "King James Version Bible" (interpreted by the Mormon church), "the Book of Mormon", and "Doctrines and Covenants". 

"Pearl of Great Price" contains a selection from revelations, translations and narrations of Joseph Smith, who is the first prophet, seer, and revelator to the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. "Pearl of Great Price" chapters include the Book of Moses, the Book of Abraham, Joseph Smith on Matthew and History and the Articles of Faith. This book is a must read for those of the mormon faith, or those studying mormon or religious history.
