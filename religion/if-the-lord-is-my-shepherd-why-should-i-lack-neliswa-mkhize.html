Once you grasp this you will know your rights as a christian.You will also know who you are in Christ Jesus. The enemy will never ever toss you around because you will take a ground and apply more word in your life. As a christian your life is a journey,you will also learn more about how to reach your final destination in depth without getting destructed. Do you know where is your final destination as a christian? After a long journey of stumbling, falling and getting up again but finally you will dwell in the house of the Lord forever and ever. It is God's will that will prevail in your life no matter what sort of destruction have you met along the way. It is God's plan to redeem and restore His people, because He cares so much for them. Our Shepherd knows His sheep by their names and they know His voice. Does the Shepherd know you by your name? Do you know the voice of the Father? Are you not confusing it with the counterfit voice? Have you struggled and tried very hard to please the Lord in your life but nothing good is happening? May be you did not need to struggle and try because, God only wants your heart to open up to Him so that He can invade and dwell within you. Once He is within your spirit, He will do the rest of the work in your life. You need to be in spirit to please the Lord, you can't try by your efforts because you not gonna be able to make it. God is reminding us that we do not have to lack anything because we've got Him as our Shepherd. All that we need is in us,it has been given to us.It grieves God that His people are perishing for the lack of knowledge(Hosea 4:6).Now is the time for the christians to get up and claim their rights and begin to live an abundant life,this is what our Lord came for on this earth not for us to suffer but to have life even more abundantly. You might have suffered lack for a long time, your life is being limited by the enemy. Find out in the book about your rights as a citizen of the kingdom of heaven.If you're a child of a king ,you don't lack anything. All that we need is there in the word of God. Right thinking, right believing and right confession. Speak the word of faith and begin to live life that you have prophecied with your mouth.
