Have you ever wanted to know why you are here? How you and God fit in with your life, and this thing called destiny and purpose?

Kevin B. Brewer examines the lives of several well-known biblical figures, and locates some uniqueness about each one that you may personally identify with yourself!

If nothing else, he allows you to ask yourself some internal questions (many of them previously indoctrinated/religious misunderstandings), about what a personal relationship with God is, or could be, or even should be.  “Faith To Obey God” is NOT about Religion, but about YOU developing YOUR BEST personal Relationship with GOD!

Life is full of challenges, but Kevin B. Brewer has developed strategies to overcome them in “Faith To Obey God.” He narrates a compelling masterpiece that is relatable and full of life’s lessons. As you read the pages inside, you will gain a greater understanding of applying “Faith To Obey God!”
