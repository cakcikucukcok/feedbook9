With the coming of Vatican ll in the early 1960s, many Catholics experienced a renewal and deepening of faith. For me this led also to a joyful experience of Gods healing power, bringing with it an increased awareness of the work of grace within our everyday lives. Gods grace transforms us, for grace is Gods way of doing the impossible within us. This book expresses something of the way we cooperate with God to receive the power of grace already ours through a life-giving union in Jesus Christ, and reflects on how throughout our lives we are dependent upon Him for the salvation of our souls.
