As a woman, have you experienced a relationship with a man you felt you couldnt let go? Do you feel the need to be in control? Have you learned about advocating for yourself after battling a life-threatening illness? Or maybe you learned to ask for help and allow others to be there for you after surviving cancer. Whatever your story, however, you have experienced the ups and downs of life, walk with the women in this book and be inspired as you gain insight into their journey. Learn about how to walk through pain and find the purpose in some experiences. It truly is possible to realize that what you thought was painful has a significant life purpose.
