My name is Wayne, and my life was one experience that youll never imagine. I grew up very poor,

and my mom had to raise fi ve children, two girls and three boys, all by herself.

But life was just diffi cult for her, and each day of my life growing up, seeing her struggle, was a

nightmare. I would sit with my Bible or alone and ask myself if there is really a god out there,

and how would this ever go awaystruggles, being poor, suffering, pain and sorrow one has to

live through in ones life. I was baptized Anglican, but from primary school, to high school, and

even when I got married, I was in a Roman Catholic church, where I was even an altar boy. Ive

experienced being and going to different types of churches, but although the preaching is somewhat

different, the beliefs are still the same. And these churches are Apostolic (new and old), Baptist,

Roman Catholic, and Anglican. On the other hand, my wife belonged to the Original Apostolic

Church, which is a combination of the New and Old Apostolic churches and Catholic churches. I

then joined their church where I became a priest, taught Sunday school, Bible classes, and went to

members home to testify and preach the gospel. Ive met a brother, who was an elder and my wifes

cousin, and two other brethren. The elder, Edward, was the one who was gifted, including myself.

We were always together, every occasion and event. Until one day we realized that every time we

went out preaching, testifying, teaching Sunday school and Bible classes, things did not make sense.

We came together each Saturday in our spare time and went over readings, through the Bible and

catechism books, and found that the Bible became very easy to understand and how we all have

been deceived, thinking that the Bible is holy. It became the book of judgment, and it shows how

mankind is falling over their feet.

Let me give an example so that we can all understand. If we read the Bible very carefully, we will

see that the Bible, even up till today, is a weapon of defense. Men went into war and battle using

the Bible or the name of their God. Today, when you enter a courtroom, the Bible is used to justify

ones wrong or right acts. How can one say the Bible is holy, yet a man does crime with all his/her

lies and must swear on the Bible? How cunning is this God that he has blinded us all. That is why

now I can see why pain, suffering, and sorrow are there because of ones disobedience.

It took seven years writing this book, which the Bible speaks of. The Book of Lifeand this is

the Predestination Salvation Plan of Christ, which is part of the Book of Life, so that we can

understand being in this body, but in full, we will understand when we leave this life, meaning

God. Because with him, there is no religion, churches, belief, day or night, male or femaleno

right or wrong, good and bad. In other words, nothing that is of God.

This is my biography, and I hope that when you are reading this book, you

will have a better and clearer understanding of what you believe. This book

is not about judging anyone, but that we should learn to respect and let go of

what we may think of others and what they believe in or not. That we accept

one another no matter what our differences may be or seem like.

You all have my blessing and have a blessed day.
