Have you ever ask God the following questions: 1. God, did you see that? 2. God, did you plan for it to happen that way?. 3. God, was it really necessary for me to go through all that? The answers to the above questions have not changed. God sees all, knows all and He has a master plan. He expects for us to lean and depend upon Him only, to put all our trust in Him, for us to use our gifts/talents to help others and for us to advance His kingdom. Let us not just be hearers of the word, Let us also be doers!!!
