Do you want to learn more about the 12 Minor Prophets in the Bible? Do you wish to uncover relevant insight for your life today?

The Bible includes the work of these 12 men who carry the unfortunate label of minor. It’s not that their work isn’t meaningful, it’s that their books are shorter. In Dear Theophilus, Minor Prophets, Peter DeHaan, PhD., a lifetime student of the Bible and founder of ABibleADay.com, digs into each one of these short books to uncover practical truths that apply to today’s followers of Jesus.

Consider Jonah. You may know that he’s the guy who spent a three-day time-out in the belly of a large fish, but what about the rest of them?

If you’re like most people, you scarcely remember their names, let alone recall what they wrote. They are Micah, Obadiah, Malachi, Nahum, Zephaniah, Zechariah, Amos, Hosea, Habakkuk, Haggai, and Joel.

In Dear Theophilus, Minor Prophets, you’ll discover:

  • The order of their ministry (because the Bible doesn’t list them chronologically)
  • The significant messages they address
  • The umbrella of hope that outshines criticism of unfaithfulness and prophecies of punishment
  • Their place in the biblical timeline
  • The powerful way their words can speak to us today
Part devotional. Part Bible study. No fluff. Totally life changing.

In book four of the Dear Theophilus series, you get all this and more: Thought-provoking insights. A deeper admiration of these lesser-known men. And a greater appreciation of how the Old Testament can inform our actions today. It’s time to dive into the intriguing lives and inspiring ministries of these amazing messengers from God.

Buy Dear Theophilus, Minor Prophets today and deepen your spiritual awareness!
