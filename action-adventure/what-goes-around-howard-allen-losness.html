Caroline Walbrook is an accomplished author whose life is about to change when she learns that the headaches she has been having and periodic passing out bouts are the result of a brain tumor that is threatening her very existence and must be surgically removed immediately.

The operation is successful, but the stress of the procedure leaves her in a coma. While in a comatose state, Carolines husband and his lover, Carolines best friend, Lenore Patterson, develop a plot to kill her. A simple drug introduced into her feeding tube, or a pillow over her face should do it, but before they can implement the plan, Caroline Walbrook comes out of her coma. She has no memory of who she is, nor does she remember anyone that had been involved in her life, including her husband. Time for plan B to be implemented.
