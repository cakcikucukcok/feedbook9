Ava Reed is a perfectly ordinary girl living in Denver, Colorado. One hour, one heart-wrenching hour, changes this entirely. Raven Monclare is a trained killer who is Avas projected sworn enemy. That is, until these two adversaries discover that they have much more in common than either would have imagined. The union of these characters in order to discover themselves and to take down the ultimate enemy makes for a novel that is actionpacked with each turn of the page. This novel explores trust and loyalty, friends and foes, life and death, and the ability to listen to ones heart when you barely know who you are yourself.
