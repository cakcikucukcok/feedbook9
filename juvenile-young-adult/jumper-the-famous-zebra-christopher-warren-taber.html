Jumper the zebra loves to jump. In fact, one night he dreams about jumping. He jumps over trees, and houses, and even a plane. He dreams he can jump over everything. In the morning, he tells his parents and grandfather about his awesome dream. They tell Jumper his dream can come true and he is capable of anything and everything he decides to do. He wants to become the world's famous zebra. Jumper works hard and practices jumping in the forest every day. He wins a competition for jumping over the moon. His work has paid off, and Jumper gains much more than a medal for his amazing jumping ability. A picture book for children, Jumper the Famous Zebra shares the story of a little zebra with big dreams who follows his grandfather's lead to great success and fulfillment with the encouragement of his parents.
