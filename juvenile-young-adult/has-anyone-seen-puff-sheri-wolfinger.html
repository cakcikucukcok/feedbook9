Billy and Jake, those mischievous pups, are at it againand this time, poor Puff, the barn cat, is caught right in the middle of it all. Puff might have thought twice about trying to sneak a taste of some of the fresh milk in Mr. Kokomos bucket if shed seen Jake sunning himself in the doorway of the barn. As Jake catches sight of Puff sneaking up to her treat his jealousy overtakes him and a whirlwind chase around the barn and beyond ensues. This adventure is likely to land both Puff and Jake in big troubleespecially when Tippy, Jakes mother, finds out what hes been up to. Billy and Jake dont look for trouble, but trouble sure seems to look for them. And on the Kokomos farm theres no shortage of adventure to be had or trouble to be gotten into. Written with children of all ages in mind, this storybook has easy-to-read sections for beginning readers and more detailed sections for advanced readers. Has Anyone Seen Puff will support your childs reading skills at any level, teaching morals and providing fun.
