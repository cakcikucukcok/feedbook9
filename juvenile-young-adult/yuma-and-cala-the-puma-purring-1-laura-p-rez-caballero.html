There is a species in the forests that lives hidden from humans. They are like us, they behave like us, they talk, they live, they feel like us, but they are not like us.

The Tupi descend from the pumas instead of descending from the monkey, and they have been living in the forests for centuries in a clandestine way to try to guarantee the survival of their species.

To achieve this, they have rules, and the most important is not to get in touch or be seen by humans.

But what happens when they find a small baby of this species abandoned and decide to keep it?

Will they be able to convince her that she is not a human or will it be impossible to escape her own origin?
