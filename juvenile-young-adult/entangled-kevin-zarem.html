Katelyn and Hannah, heres a line from one of your favorite movies, The Notebook: You know what they always say, doc? Science only goes so far, and then theres God. In a nutshell, this is what my book is trying to say. I would add, however, that there exists an elegant bridge connecting the physical world of science and the metaphysical world of God, and this bridge is in our minds.

I think our true purpose in life is to find this bridgeto become awareto be with God. To date, I have struggled mightily at this, but I know that my unconditional love for each of you has me moving in exactly the right direction.
