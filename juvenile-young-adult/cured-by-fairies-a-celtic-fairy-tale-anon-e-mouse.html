ISSN: 2397-9607 Issue 141

In this 141st story in the Baba Indaba’s Children's Stories series, Baba Indaba narrates the Celtic story of a sage-femme, a midwife, who is summoned by the Dark Rider and taken across the mortal divide to tend the fairy queen who is about to give birth. When she arrives, she is given a warning……. Download and read this story to find out what the warning was and if the midwife was tempted to ignore it - or not.

Baba Indaba is a fictitious Zulu storyteller who narrates children's stories from around the world. Baba Indaba translates as "Father of Stories".

Each issue also has a "WHERE IN THE WORLD - LOOK IT UP" section, where young readers are challenged to look up a place on a map somewhere in the world. The place, town or city is relevant to the story, on map. HINT - use Google maps.
