THE RIWAKA GANG© – FOUR COUNTRY KIDS INTO THRILLING ADVENTURES

A holiday trip at an uncle’s cattle station, to help the annual roundup becomes a terrifying adventure as The Riwaka Gang; four country kids; Jackson, Jessica, Rangi and Sam suddenly find themselves in the midst of a spine-tingling drama. They will need every skill they possess plus their knowledge of survival in the New Zealand mountains to escape – not only the perils of nature but also the many dangers they encounter.

With so many impossibilities, the odds are stacked high against them. Can they survive? The story is set in the high mountain ranges of New Zealand – in the South Pacific.
