In Free To Be Me, we meet Moriah a trusting, innocent baby elephant who looks forward to unwrapping each days adventures with enthusiasm and wonderment. One evening in a dream, Moriah learns that life may have some unexpected circumstances and unforeseen forces that wish to hold her back from pursuing the life she was created to live.

In a moment of despair Moriah is visited by the wind which teaches her the purpose and the meaning of her life. She learns that God made her for a purpose which only she can fulfill. Can Moriah find the courage and faith to follow Gods dream for her life?

My hope is that Free to Be Me will encourage readers of all ages to pursue Gods dream for their lives with the wonder, fascination and trusting faith of a child.
