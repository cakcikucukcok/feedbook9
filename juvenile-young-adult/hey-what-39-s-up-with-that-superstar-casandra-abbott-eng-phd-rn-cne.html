The Alley Pal Adventure Series addresses barriers in pediatric health and empowers children to make more informed choices by instilling the fundamental concepts of health and wellness. This series transforms the childs perspective as the alley pals, empowered with knowledge, make better choices which in turn lead to better health. The Alley Pals will captivate the hearts and minds of young readers while guiding them on a path to better health. Second in the series, Gold Medals to Black Lungs, encourages young readers to choose a lifestyle free of smoking. Disclaimer: The purpose of this book is to educate the reader on the dangers of smoking. The author is not suggesting that every smoker will be diagnosed with lung cancer.
