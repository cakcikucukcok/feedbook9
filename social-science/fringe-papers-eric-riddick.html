EVEN IN THE DARKNESS OF A FULL ECLIPSE, THE STARS STILL SHINE! A LESSON SELF TAUGHT IS TRULY A LESSON BEST TAUGHT! IN THIS DAY AND AGE WE FIND THAT IT HAS ALWAYS BEEN THOSE MINDS WHO FOUND IT NECESSARY TO TEACH THEMSELVES THE TRUTH OF A MATTER, THAT ULTIMATELY GO ON TO REVOLUTIONIZE THE WORLD WITH THE GROUND BREAKING TRUTHS THAT THOSE MINDS CAME TO REALIZE IN THEIR JOURNEY TO TRUTH. AND NOW, HERE WE HAVE ANOTHER OF THOSE MINDS WITH A NEW GROUND BREAKING TRUTH TO DELIVER TO THE WORLD AT THE VERY TIME IT IS TRULY NEEDED THE MOST. WHILE UNJUSTLY INCARCERATED WELL OVER TWO DECADES FOR A CRIME HE DID NOT COMMIT, THIS REVOLUTIONARY MIND, KNOWN TO MANY AS BROTHER GENERAL (ERIC RIDDICK) NOT ONLY CONSTRUCTIVELY UTILIZED HIS TIME TO UNRAVEL AND TENACIOUSLY FIGHT AGAINST THE INJUSTICES BEING PERPETUATED AGAINST HIM AND MANY OTHERS BY A BROKEN JUDICIAL SYSTEM, BUT ALSO TO RE-EDUCATE HIMSELF IN THE SCIENCE OF LIFE, HEALTH AND WELL BEING. NOW WITH MATHEMATICAL PRECISION AND CAUSE AND EFFECT PRACTICALITY, BROTHER GENERAL (ERIC RIDDICK) HAS REALIZED AND REVEALED TO US, THE MECHANICAL DYNAMICS OF THE UNDERLYING CAUSE OF THE MANY RNA MOLECULAR ABERRATIONS THAT ACTS AS VIRUSES AND DISEASES IN HUMAN BIOLOGICAL SYSTEMS. WITH CONCISE, EASILY VERIFIABLE FACTS, SOUND WISDOM AND THOUGHT FOR THOUGHT KNOWLEDGE, BROTHER GENERAL (ERIC RIDDICK) PRESENTS TO US, FUNDAMENTAL THOUGHTS ON RNA EXPRESSIONS AND ACTIVITY AS IT RELATES TO THESE DISEASES & VIRUSES COMMONLY CALLED CANCER, AIDS/HIV ALONG WITH SEVERAL OTHER RNA MOLECULAR ALIMENTS. HERE IN THIS WRITING, APTLY TITLED FRINGE PAPERS, BROTHER GENERAL HAS ORGANIZED AND MADE AVAILABLE FOR THE WORLD, THE TRUE AND LIVING UNDERSTANDING OF OPTIMAL HEALTH AND WELL BEING THAT HAS BEEN WELL OVER DUE. THIS WRITING MAY VERY WELL BE THE MOST SIGNIFICANT, MOST VALUABLE, MOST PURPOSEFUL AND MOST IMPORTANT ADDITION TO THE VERY HEALTH AND WELL BEING OF THE WORLD SINCE THE DISCOVERY OF PENICILLIN.
