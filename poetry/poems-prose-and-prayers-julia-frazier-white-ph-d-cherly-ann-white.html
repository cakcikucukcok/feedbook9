Poems, Prose, and Prayers by Julia Frazier White, PhD, is a collection of original poems, prose, favorite scriptures and their meanings in her life, and prayers. In her writing, she shares her thoughts and feelings as a legacy to her children, her grandchildren, her friends, and extended family. It is hoped that all will fi nd a favorite here. Dr. Whites mother collected poems and taught Julia and her siblings to memorize poetry and speeches and to recite them to her father and other audiences. Included are prayers that have served to increase Julias faith and, hopefully, the readers faith, too.
