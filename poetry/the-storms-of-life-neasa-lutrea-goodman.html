I am the youngest of 8 siblings in my family. The daughter of Jesse and Teressie Goodman. I was born on January 14, 1970 in Dothan, Alabama, and graduated from Northview High School in 1988. I attended College for nursing and I am a member of Allen Temple Church of God In Christ. I currently stay in Dothan, Alabama as the mother of four talented children. My writing began when I was 32 years old and encountered many troubles in my life due to divorce, raising kids as a single mother, and attending college for my nursing degree. As time went on I just began writing about many topics that touched me in many different ways.
