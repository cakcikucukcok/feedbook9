Prepare yourself for an emotional adventure that will carry your thoughts from lifes low points to the limits of a cloud-less sky. Take a ride on this rollercoaster of excitement that will navigate you around the bumps of life. Through thoughts of death, love, hope, family and relationships, your emotions will continually be shaken as you ride the ups and downs and unforeseen curves. You will scream for more as you shed tears of sadness and joy. From the emotions of losing a loved one to the joy of finding a new love you will be touched like never before.

This wide assortment of poetry will expose your mind to the emotions of life, lifes poor decisions and your inner fears that are not easily spoken of out loud. We all have special gifts, the greatest of which is our willingness to open ourselves up so that others may be comforted. Gasp this opportunity to face your inner thoughts and allow yourself to be guided by the thoughts from within.
