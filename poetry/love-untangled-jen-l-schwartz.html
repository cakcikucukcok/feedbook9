Love Untangled is the debut poetry collection from published poet Jen L. Schwartz.  She is known on Instagram for her emotive poetry about life, love, existence, relationships and the daily struggles of the human condition.  Love Untangled explores the complexity of human behavior, particularly, the connection between souls, life, love and creativity.  Her poetry uses the art of words to explore the beautiful chaos of human relationships and the ever-burning need for something beyond our own existence. Language has a power beyond comprehension, from the healing of love and forgiveness to the damage left by words left unspoken.  The right words can make us fly, to escape the prison of our own design. So come along for the ride.
