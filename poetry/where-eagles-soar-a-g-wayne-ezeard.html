Experience the excitement of the rodeo in A Bull named Rock and Roll, follow the adventures of Wes Morgan in Where Eagles Soar, and laugh over the rude awakening of Ben, the lovesick cowboy, in Corra Leah. These stories and poems depict the pioneering spirit in all their many facets; laughter and tears, triumph and despair.
