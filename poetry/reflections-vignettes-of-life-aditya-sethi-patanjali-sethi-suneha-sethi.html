Celebrating lifemagical, wondrous, captivating, ironic! It inspires, provokes comment on events, incidents, social issues, lifes facets in todays world. We are an uncompromising, integral part of lifes journey, its philosophy, cynicism, and positivity. Their poignancy touches a chord, causing you to feel the poems and appreciate them as they mirror life. Readers will easily identify with and relate to many poems, where you may just see your life reflected in them. Their poems, transcending three generations, take you into a world of varied writing styles, themes, which encapsulates their vision, opinions on what life holds for them. They are philosophical, showcasing life in all its color, splendor, and magnitude. Sometimes cynical, they turn positive while covering lifes differing aspects. The poems speaking mostly about lifes events, daily happenings, social matters, also question our very existence. Terrorism, the regular grind, lifes facades are projected in their inimitable poetic styles. Vibrant, energetic-the poems are filled with thoughts on why things are what they are. They provoke you to ask yourself questions that may or may not provide you with acceptable answers. Poems that are expressive, if sometimes abstract, reflect a true account of life today.
