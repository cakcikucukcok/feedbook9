Embarking on a life-altering journey through the green fields of an extrardinary festival, a young songwriter changes the course of her life for good when she is faced with the truth of herself and the possibilities of the question: What would my life look like if it was guided by love?

Featuring lyrics from some of the UK festival scene's most loved songwriters (Mike Scott, Praying for the Rain, Carrie Tree, Michael Stillwater, Seize the Day and many others), Medicine Song is an invitation to dance in the Water, Fire, Air and Earth of this reality and to wake up to the true magic and mystery at the centre of every one of us and the power of the human heart to heal and create real change.

We all chose to be here at this time for a reason...
