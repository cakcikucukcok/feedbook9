From royal nanny to princess bride?

Dedicated teacher Kayleen James was determined to safeguard the future of her orphaned students – even if it meant defying Prince As’ad of El Deharia himself! But the seductive ruler stunned her by offering to adopt the three little girls. On one condition… Suddenly As’ad was a single father in desperate need of a nanny, and Kayleen was the only one for the job.

Soon the palace was in an uproar – all because of this spirited redhead. Losing his heart wasn’t part of the arrangement, but then Kayleen showed the honour-bound sheikh what he’d been missing.

Could he convince her that she belonged in his desert kingdom as his princess – and his wife?
