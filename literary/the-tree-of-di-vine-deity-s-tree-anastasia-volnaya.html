The philosophical parable "The Tree of Di-Vine", written in 1997. Included in the cycle of prose works by Anastasia Volnaya under the general title "Time". The parable is one of the attempts to comprehend our earthly existence, through the fantasy characters of the winged Lan and the inhabitants of the fictitious country - bezoffs.

This parable complements or transforms the lyrical parable-tale " On the other side of the rainbow," which I hope will also be of interest to my readers.

In addition, the book presents two options for writing a philosophical parable: in English and in Russian.
