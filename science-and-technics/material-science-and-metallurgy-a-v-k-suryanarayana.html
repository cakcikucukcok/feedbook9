The content of Material Science and Metallurgy is purely metallurgical. The syllabus is covered by the author who is a metallurgist. About the Author:

A.V.K.Suryanarayana is a metallurgist from the famous Banaras Hindu University, he has thirty five years of teaching and industrial experience. He has successfully authored two other books namely, Testing of Metallic Materials and Fuels, Furnaces, Refractories and Pyrometry, which are well received.
