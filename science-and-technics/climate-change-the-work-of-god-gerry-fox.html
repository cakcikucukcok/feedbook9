Climate change is a terrible problem and the constant argument is always on manmade industrial pollution as the culprit. This, however, fails to take into consideration the Bible's explanation which is far-reaching. In fact, did you know that practically every book of the Old Testament and a few of the New Testament books mention climate change? We have a pattern unfolding and to truly understand the what and why of it all is for us to return to the words of the bible. Come follow this writer into a discovery which will leave you amazed and blessed, one that can even provide the solution to our weather woes.
