The year is 2030, the United States has gone through a radical change. Only government agencies have access to the internet and cell phones. The morality of the country is non-existent. However, it is also a time of great discoveries in the field of medicine. Yet those are overshadowed by greed. In all major cities there is a section called Sex Town. A place where anyone can get anything of a sexual nature and its legal. Streets have been renamed and moved to make room for these places. The story follows the investigation of a reporter named Noah Wolfe. He is investigating the deaths and mutilations of young teenage prostitutes. You will follow him through his investigation, knowing only what he sees, hears, and thinks.
