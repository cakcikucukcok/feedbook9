The body of an unidentified woman is discovered along a trail, her infant child next to her, alive and now alone in the world. As the child grows it, becomes clear she has exceptional intelligence, but she suffers from somnambulism - sleepwalking.

The child’s episodes become progressively more traumatic until she is eventually committed to a mental health institute, where she attracts the attentions of others.

As the child explores her capabilities, she starts to wonder who she really is, and takes control of her own destiny. But as events unfold, who is seeking to control whom?

SLEEP is a psychological thriller that follows the discovery of an exceptional girl, who seemingly surrounded by tragedy, sets out to take control of her own life.
