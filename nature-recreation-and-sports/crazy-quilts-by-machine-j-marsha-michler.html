Create a Breathtaking Heirloom

Known for their lavish embellishment, bold colors, and refined composition, crazy quilts are Victoriana at its best. Crazy quilts are traditionally pieced and embellished with hand methods, but now you - regardless of your skill level - have an all-in-one resource for creating crazy quilts solely by machine or by combining hand and machine methods. Besides providing four machine piecing and patching methods, this lavishly illustrated volume includes:

  • An encyclopedia of machine embellishments suitable for any machine that will do straight and zigzag stitches
  • Complete instructions for 20 original, unique crazy quilted projects
  • Designs for bed, wall, and children's quilts, throws, table covers and pillows
  • Full-size templates
