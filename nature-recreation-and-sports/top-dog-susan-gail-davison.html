This is a true story of how we came to have a wonderful dog as part of our family. It's a story about an orphan puppy that becomes top dog in a family that adopts him and loves him.

The name Sgt Major is considered to be Top Dog in the army. The homeless dog in the animal rescue shelter needed a family and they esteemed him to be the best dog they could ever ask for. Our family made him a top dog because he was so loving and turned out to be the most rewarding of all the dogs we have ever owned.

My children and I thought it was time to find a good home for our baby bunny rabbits that we had raised in our back yard. So we brought them to the local animal rescue shelter to be adopted by a family that wants to have a loving pet. That is where you go when you want to adopt a pet. We saw so many cats and dogs while we were there that needed to be adopted to a good and loving home.

Our hearts were filled with compassion as we looked at each one. All the animals looked so sad and scared without having a good home of their own.

It is a very happy ending when you can pick a lost animal to bring home.

Everyone is happy. When we love animals they fill our hearts with joy. Animals are very happy too when they are cared for and loved by their owner's.
