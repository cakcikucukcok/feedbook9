DACHSHUND AND DACHSHUNDS is your definitive Dachshund Guide! Everything is covered: from Dachshund Puppies, Dachshund Breeders, Dachshund Grooming, Dachshund Training, bringing your Dachshund home, Dachshund Dogs as Adults, Dachshund Health & Nutrition, Dachshund Temperament, and all the proven INSIDER TIPS you’ll need to have the very best experience with your Dachshund!

This one-stop resource will help you to care for, train, & build a successful partnership with your Dachshund, & makes a great gift for new owners and experienced Dachshund lovers.

Canine author Susanne Saben has produced the most in-depth & up-to-date book yet on the Dachshund. The 118 pages are easy to read & packed full of practical info & breeders' tips on everything you need to know about getting to know, caring for & living with the magnificent Dachshund! Everything is Covered – including:

Dachshund Puppies – You’ll Learn:

  • How to prepare Your Home & Yard
  • What to do when Your puppy first arrives
  • Dachshund Sleeping Arrangements
  • Getting through the first week
  • Exercise – how much and what type
  • Crate training for your Dachshund
  • Housetraining
  • Socialization – what does it mean and how to do it right
  • Dacshund Training and Obedience
  • How to avoid the missteps which could make Your Dachshund’s personality not turn out right.
Dachshund And Dachshunds is full of tried-and-tested personalized advice on what to expect & all aspects of life with your Dachshund, from bringing home your puppy through adolescence- and into a thriving adulthood.

For anyone who hasn’t chosen their Dachshund yet there is:

  • Dachshund variations and color patterns.
  • Dachshund hybrids and how to tell Purebred from Mixes
  • The Personality of the Dachshund vs Other Competing Breeds
  • Dachshund Breeders- What to look for (and to AVOID!).
  • Where NOT to Buy a Dachshund
  • How to Pick a Healthy Dachshund Puppy or Dachshund Adult
  • Temperament: Quickly understanding the Temperament of the exact Dachshund You’re Considering
  • Dachshund Socialization- Preventing Anti-Social Behavior
Learn about Dachshund Food-What breeders recommend, Feeding Puppies, Raw Diets, Avoiding Food Allergies, Understanding Dog Food Labels, & Feeding Older Dogs.

In Depth- Dachshund Health: including Ears, Skin, Allergies, Spaying, Neutering, Vaccinations, Preventing Fleas & Microchipping, Dachshund common illnesses and how to avoid them!

Renowned world expert advice on the Dachshund including grooming & caring for the Dachshund coat & skin, and the BEST activities to make them shine!

PS: This is not a run-of-the-mill book… it is for true Dachshund lovers- full of real world advice on how to make the most of your Dachshund’s life and journey together!
