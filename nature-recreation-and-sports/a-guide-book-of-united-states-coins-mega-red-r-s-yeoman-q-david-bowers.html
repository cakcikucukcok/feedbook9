MEGA RED is the new expanded version of the hobby’s best-selling GUIDE BOOK OF UNITED STATES COINS (the RED BOOK), with more pricing in more grades than the regular edition, plus certified population data, more die varieties, and more auction records. It covers American coinage from colonial times to the modern day—copper tokens and half cents through $20 gold double eagles, plus bullion, commemoratives, Proof and Mint sets, significant patterns and medals, error coins, and other collectibles. It has everything you need to know about U.S. coins. Expanded coverage of colonial coins and tokens. More background on private and territorial gold pieces. Expanded history and explanation of Philippine/U.S. coinage. Additional information on Civil War tokens, pattern coins, Hawaiian and Puerto Rican coinage, Alaska tokens, So-Called Dollars, special modern gold, dime scrip, “paper dimes,” sutler dime tokens, dimes from the SS Central America, the 1894-S dime, a snapshot of the dime market in 1946, irradiated dimes, and more.
