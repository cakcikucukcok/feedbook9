The Origamido Studio and world renowned origami artists Michael G. LaFosse and Richard H. Alexander, introduce one of their most comprehensive origami paper craft kits, Origami Spectacular! This packaged origami kit has just about everything paper folders of any age or level of skill need to create masterful 3D origami models. The projects range from beginner origami, origami-for-kids folds, to somewhat more challenging, mid-level folds making this kit a great way to learn origami. The scope of projects and copious amounts of origami paper means you will you trouble running out of new projects.

The paper craft kit includes:

  • A full-color booklet
  • Clear step-by-step instructions
  • Easy-to-follow diagrams
  • 60 fun and simple projects
  • Traditional designs as well as new origami models.
  • 154 sheets of authentic origami paper
  • A range of colors and sizes
Presented in an attractive package, this kit a a great value and makes a wonderful gift for anyone interested in origami or arts and crafts.

  • Whiskers the Cat

  • Peacock
  • Jumping Frog
  • Flapping Bird

  • Snapdragon
  • And many more
