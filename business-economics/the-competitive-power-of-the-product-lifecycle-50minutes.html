The fundamental stages behind every product

This book is a practical and accessible guide to understanding and applying the concept of product lifecycle, providing you with the essential information and saving time. 

In 50 minutes you will be able to: 

   o Understand the theory of the product lifecycle and the features of each of the four phases: launch, growth, maturity and decline 

   o  Learn how to make the right decisions according to the lifecycle phases of your product and how to react during the decline phase 

   o Identify how you can use the product lifecycle to tailor your marketing strategies and constantly innovate and improve your product in order to prolong its growth phase
