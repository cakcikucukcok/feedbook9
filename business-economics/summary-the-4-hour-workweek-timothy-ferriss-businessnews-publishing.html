Complete summary of  Timothy Ferriss' book:  "The 4-Hourweek: Escape 9-5, Live Anywhere, and Join the New Rich"

The review of the ideas in Timothy Ferriss' book:  "The 4-Hourweek" shows that there is a new subculture of people in the world who have figured out that the 9-5 grindstone doesn’t really fire up their imaginations to any great degree. Instead, they organize their lives and follow a set of uncommon rules which typically allows them to work less than four hours a week but earn more in a month than most people do in a year.  These people are the "New Rich" (NR). They abandon the conventional deferred-life plan (work now and retire later) and instead develop their own signature lifestyles which utilize the currencies of the New Rich: time and mobility.This summary highlights the four steps to follow which will take you from being deferred-life oriented to becoming part of the New Rich.
