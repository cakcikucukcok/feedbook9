Written for mothers everywhere who seek to fulfill their purpose in every aspect of their personal and professional lives, the ten stories in this anthology carve out inspiring and practical paths to reaching the exalted status of #BossMom!  

Every author is a boss in her own right, specializing in a certain area of success: one mom will teach you the tenets of unbeatable customer service, while another will show you how she engages her own children in entrepreneurship and financial literacy. Others will help you cultivate your winning mindset, uncover your purpose, build strong relationships, and still be the best family woman you can be.  

Mompreneurship leaves no woman behind. Read, engage, learn from, and trust in these testimonies—if you have the will, the courage, and the desire to see better lives for yourself and your loved ones, you are already in the making of a Boss Mom!
