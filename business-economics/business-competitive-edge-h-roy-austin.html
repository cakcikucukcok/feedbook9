Do you want to grow your business, increase profits and cash flow, and make your organization more efficient? Here’s the user manual you've been looking for.

 

Businesses Competitive Edge – The Alligator Business Solution enables business owners and aspiring entrepreneurs to manage their key functions and make better decisions. You don’t have to be an expert in each function but like it or not, you must manage them all and to do that you need to know where to look, what to look for, and how to use the information.

Eighteen successful business were interviewed and you will benefit from their experiences. Through humor, unique graphics, and true-life stories, positive & negative, you’ll learn how to build your business house in the business swamp, establish a solid foundation, gain an understanding of your most important business functions, and align your decisions and business functions with your foundation. In the process, you will learn tools and concepts to grow your businesses, make it more profitable, improve cash flow, and streamline operations, i.e., gain a competitive edge.

This book will benefit entrepreneurs, start-ups, existing businesses, and organizations of all sizes and types both professionally and personally.
