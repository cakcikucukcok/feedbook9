Millennial women are living in a time when career opportunities have never been better—if you want them, that is. In the past, most people accepted the necessity of “putting in the time.” You had to earn your way up the corporate ladder or hope to be in the right place when the next opportunity presented itself. Michelle Turman shows you how to seize those opportunities, achieve great things, and get what you want.

In Jumping the Queue: Achieving Great Things Before You Are Ready, you will learn to:

  • Recognize and avoid the detractors that are hindering you from believing in yourself.
  • Overcome personal or professional adversity that may be holding you back from moving forward.
  • Learn how to understand your moral compass, tell your story and grow your reputation wisely so you can live your true purpose.
