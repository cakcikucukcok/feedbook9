Modern Science has changed significantly over the last 10 years. What reckoned to be the only universal true is rigorously challenged by the modern researches nowadays. We are experiencing shift in paradigms we were taught in schools and the Science evolves further. Accordingly, the Medicine changes significantly, too. Not only the robotic surgery and genetic engineering invented would change the shape of the modern medicine, but also the knowledge each individual acquire, would contribute to the evolution of our society to the new one, where no diseases, nor poverty, neither any sort of abuse would survive, as they would extinguished by the principles of natural laws; the principles that are already work and always have worked at every level of our existence. All we need to do is to aware of those basic principles and to NOT HARM.
