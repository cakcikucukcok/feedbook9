Frank, honest and pulling no punches, ‘Are You Happy?’ launches you on a journey to find the forgotten you – the real you that wants to be happy. Very clearly, step-by-step, it defines Happiness, explains what makes you tick and then details exactly how you can earn Happiness.

In other words, ‘Are You Happy?’ provides you with the recipe for the most wonderful treat of your life – Happiness.
