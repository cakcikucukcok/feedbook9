Are you ready for change in your life?

Tired of living up to others expectations?

In A Year and Some Change: Revealing Your Full Potential through Purpose and Perspective, Reginald Bean challenges you to take the next year and a few additional months to look at yourself, explore what you expect from yourself and from life, and answer the question, "What great contribution will I have on my community and family?" He encourages you to pick one area of your life, community, or social circle to change and explains how to do it. Through a series of resolutions, personal anecdotes, and creative exercises, Reginald Bean will guide your way to revealing your full potential.
