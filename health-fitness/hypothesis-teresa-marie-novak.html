My Hypothesis

I can heal myself from stage-zero cervical cancer, at age 50, while keeping my cervix, my heart and my mind intact. Leveraging methods I learned throughout my career in software engineering, I designed my own healing experiment to prove out this hypothesis.

One Process - Infinite Possibilities

In this book, we walk together through my own decision-making process that is based on the scientific method and augmented with the power heart and mind. This can be a guide to any problem-solving really: but for our purpose now in addressing HPV and cervical dysplasia, this process is used to define your own personal experiment in self-healing.

My Experiment

Part 1: The treatment plan I designed for myself required a commitment to incorporate alternative medicine into my everyday life. I approached this as my own personal experiment in self-healing, and measured my progress with standard cervical cancer screenings.

Procedures

  • Naturopathic medicine: anti-inflammatory diet, supplements and natural topical treatments.
  • Yoga practice: breathe (pranayama), movement (asana), meditation, and mantra.
  • Thank you: create a vision of gratitude transforming all expected results into tangible actions of being grateful.
  • Measurement: Cancer progression screenings according to the ACOG schedule of Well-Women ages 40-65.

Your Own Healing Experiment

You are not alone in this! Every year in America there are 250,000 to a million women who receive a cervical dysplasia diagnosis.  In Part 2 of the book, we walk through the decision-making process that facilitates the development of your own healing experiment. The foundation is to apply self-observation to understand your own Joy and make actionable decisions. Each step of the way I provide plenty of resources: my story as an example, references including materials lists, space for journaling your findings and thoughts and words of experience from women just like you who have faced the same choices.
