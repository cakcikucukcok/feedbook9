"Hugs", is a book for children, with a message for mothers; a book to read and enjoy together. Born from the pen of Fabiola Piccolo and the watercolors of Sofia Peotta, a mother in close contact with her children, and an expert in 'babywearing'. "Hugs" is a sweet tale about maternity, about fabric that binds and about the bond of love. Narrated by a child carried in a baby wrap from birth, growing up in close physical contact with her mother. The objective of this book is to talk about the daily life of parents who carry their children, and to promote baby wearing as a child-rearing practice.
