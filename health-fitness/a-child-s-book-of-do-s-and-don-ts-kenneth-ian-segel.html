We all want our children and grandchildren to do well and be good. We want them to live productive lives that will fulfill them and earn our respect and pride. A Childs Book of Dos and Donts is a selected listing of worthy virtues and timeless values. It offers numerous specific guidelines to build character and enrich living. From being yourself and liking yourself to accepting peoples differences and appreciating their good, from giving your best effort and being a proud American to being thankful for all you have and getting the most out of each day, the books message is practical, relevant, and necessary. The setting is modern. The message is classic. This books wisdom will remain fresh and grow in meaning through the years.
