This book is intended to encourage readers on matters that affect all of us. The focus is on the fundamental truths of life, which you can apply even if your purpose or place in life seems uncertain. I took the opportunity to share my experiences with life struggles and conflicts. I wish to provide an awareness of the difficulties and issues that people face daily yet take for granted or neglect amidst the noise and confusion of modern life. I hope you will find inner peace through these inspirational thoughts, in spite of what you may be going through. Dont be disheartened by situations that make you feel worthless or make you want to give up. I hope that you will find solace and comfort through these writings. May you find the will to gain new heights and accomplish great things. I am blessed to share the testimonies of how I strive to overcome the obstacles to accomplish my vision. No matter what, you can get past your past.
