Want a new job or career… but feeling “trapped” in a current line of work? 

Craving a new career... but afraid to make the leap?

Now may be just the time to cut through the overwhelm and get the next chapter in motion. This Life Guide can help.

Taking that first step can be scary, but you have more than enough strength to do it. 

Inside this Life Guide on How To Find Work That You Love you'll find practical steps to inspire you to take those first few steps towards a more meaningful career.

You'll learn how to:

  • Uncover your true aspirations (they might not be what you think!)
  • Discover your career desires and non-negotiable “dealbreakers.”
  • Step forward into work that reflects your deepest aspirations.
  • Manage your emotions, deal with overwhelm, fear and job-hunt rejection, and stay positive, even when things aren’t happening as fast as you'd like.
  • Uplift your self-talk, build confidence and stop procrastinating using scripts and/or worksheets.
  • And more!
Join the many other people who have learned how to take those tough first few steps towards a more meaningful career by following the principles in How To Find Work That You Love today!
